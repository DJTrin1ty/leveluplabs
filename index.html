Overview

Level Up Labs's Bootcamp Prep is a part-time program that begins on the first Monday of every month and lasts for the next 4 consecutive weeks. Students can expect a minimum 60 hours of onsite learning with remote work to be completed on off-nights. Due to the intensity of this program, we only recommend it to those that are truly committed and are willing to dedicate their time and efforts into attending a top tier bootcamp.
